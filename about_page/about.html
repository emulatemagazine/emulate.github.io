EMULATE

Our Mission

(same message as this semester's magazine, need to change?) Emulate — An act of transforming: a striving to become. The arts open space for potential, for the newly becoming, for evolution. In art, we strive to be equal to our potential and to surpass it. Bringing the power of the arts we love into the everyday, Emulate celebrates the striving and the becoming of the Pioneer Valley. Here, let’s fix ourselves on those who inspire us. Let’s emulate them. Let’s create.
